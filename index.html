About NTAC

Why Train With NTAC?

When you train with NTAC, you're learning from someone who has actually used these skills under pressure. Todd Nielsen spent over 25 years in law enforcement, served on Special Operations units, and provided protective detail for two U.S. Presidents. He built NTAC on one belief: that every responsible citizen deserves access to the same level of training trusted by the military and law enforcement. You'll leave every class more capable, more confident, and more prepared than when you walked in.
