Handgun Proficiency Class

Draw, Move, Shoot — All Before the Target Reaches You

Reserve My Spot

This class is for shooters who can already hit a target and are ready to do more than stand still. Handgun Proficiency builds the skills that matter outside the static range: drawing from a holster, shooting on the move, and staying accurate when the pressure is on. Not quite ready? Start with Beginner Handgun first.

What You'll Master

Skills You'll Take With You

✓
Drawing from a holster with speed, control, and consistency every time
✓
Shooting accurately while moving in any direction — forward, lateral, and back
✓
Magazine changes and reloads without breaking your focus or losing your target
✓
Firing from behind cover with proper technique and situational awareness
✓
The muscle memory that holds up under pressure, not just on a calm range
Who This Is For

Ready To Move Beyond the Static Range?

Shooters who have completed Beginner Handgun and are ready to push further. You should be able to hit a stationary target consistently before taking this class.

If you're not sure whether you're ready, ask yourself: can you hit a paper target reliably at 7 yards? If yes, this class is your next step.

What to Expect

Dynamic Drills From Day One.

  • Holster draws and shooting on the move from the first session
  • Every skill is coached from the ground up — nothing assumed
  • Complexity ramps as your confidence grows throughout the day
  • Close instructor supervision on a live fire controlled range
  • You'll leave with a comfort level that a static range simply can't build

What to Bring

  • Properly functioning handgun
  • At least 3 magazines for your handgun
  • Minimum of 250 rounds of practice ammo
  • A holster that covers the trigger guard completely
  • Eye protection
  • Electronic hearing protection
  • Closed-toe shoes, long pants, high neckline shirt
