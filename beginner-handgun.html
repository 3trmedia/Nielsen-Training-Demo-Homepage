Beginner Handgun Class

Learn To Shoot — No Prior Experience Needed

Reserve My Spot

If you've never shot before or just want to feel more comfortable around a firearm, this is the right starting point. Everyone in the room is at the same level, the pace is relaxed, and our instructors make safety the foundation of everything.

What You'll Master

Skills You'll Take With You

✓
Fundamental firearm safety — the habits that keep everyone safe on and off the range
✓
Grip, stance, and trigger control that actually produces accurate shots
✓
How to use your sights and aim with real confidence at the target
✓
Defensive targeting — where to aim and why it matters in a real scenario
✓
The basic legal principles of armed self-defense in the state of Utah
Who This Is For

Starting From Zero? You're In the Right Place.

Anyone starting from zero. You don't need prior experience or even your own gun. If firearms make you nervous, that's exactly the kind of person this class was designed for.

Patty and the NTAC instructors have trained hundreds of first-time shooters. The zero-judgment atmosphere is real — ask every question you have.

What to Expect

Classroom First. Range Second. Leave Confident.

  • We start in the classroom before you ever touch a firearm
  • You'll learn how your handgun works and how to handle it safely under zero pressure
  • Then you move to live fire with hands-on coaching for every shot
  • Most students arrive nervous and leave proud of what they accomplished
  • Class runs approximately half a day — no experience required to register

What to Bring

  • Properly functioning handgun
  • At least 2 magazines (holding 20+ rounds total capacity)
  • Minimum of 150 rounds of practice ammo
  • Eye protection
  • Electronic hearing protection
  • Closed-toe shoes, long pants, high neckline shirt
