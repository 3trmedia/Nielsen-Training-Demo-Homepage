Rifle Proficiency Class

Stop Standing Still. Learn To Run Your Rifle.

Reserve My Spot

Rifle Proficiency takes the fundamentals you built in Beginner Rifle and puts them in motion. You'll work through dynamic drills, shooting on the move, rapid magazine changes, and engaging targets from multiple positions — all live fire, all coached. This is where your rifle skills stop being static and start being useful. Not quite ready? Start with Beginner Rifle first.

What You'll Master

Skills You'll Take With You

✓
Shooting accurately while moving in multiple directions with a loaded rifle
✓
Rapid magazine changes and reloads without breaking your shooting rhythm
✓
Engaging multiple targets quickly and efficiently under pressure
✓
Firing from prone, kneeling, and standing positions with speed and accuracy
✓
Building muscle memory through repetitive live fire drills with instructor feedback
Who This Is For

Ready To Move Beyond the Static Range?

This course is for shooters who have completed Beginner Rifle and are comfortable with the fundamentals. If you can safely handle your rifle and hit a stationary target, you're ready to take the next step.

Rifle Proficiency is where good shooters get better — and static shooters start moving.

What to Expect

Dynamic Drills From Day One.

  • Movement-based live fire drills begin in the first session
  • Coaching on technique for every position and drill
  • Timed scenarios designed to build speed without sacrificing accuracy
  • Immediate, practical feedback from NTAC instructors
  • Plan on 300–400 rounds — come ready to work

What to Bring

  • Rifle or carbine with a sling
  • At least 3 magazines for your rifle
  • 300–400 rounds — no armor-piercing or steel core ammo
  • Eye protection
  • Hearing protection
  • Closed-toe shoes, long pants, high neckline shirt
