Rifle Proficiency Class

Stop Standing Still. Learn To Run Your Rifle.

Reserve My Spot

You can hit a target with your rifle, but can you do it while moving? This class teaches you what comes next. Our Rifle Proficiency Class is built around one core skill: handling your rifle effectively while you move. If you're not yet able to shoot beyond the static range, this is where you level up. Not quite there yet? Start with our Beginner Rifle course first.

Skills You'll Master:

  • Shooting accurately from standing, kneeling, seated, and prone positions
  • Moving with your rifle and staying on target
  • Transitioning between positions without losing accuracy
  • Magazine changes and reloads on the move
  • Building the muscle memory that holds up under pressure

Ready to run your rifle?

Most rifle shooters practice from a standing position, aiming at a stationary target. That's a good starting point, but not a practical skill set.

This course is built around one principle: good technique doesn't stop when you start moving. You'll train from multiple positions — standing, kneeling, seated, and prone — learning to transition between them while maintaining accuracy. Every drill adds complexity as your confidence grows, always under close instructor supervision on a controlled range.

By the end of the course you'll have a level of comfort and capability with your rifle that a static range simply can't teach you.
