Beginner Rifle Class

Learn To Shoot a Rifle. No Experience Needed.

Reserve My Spot

Whether you just picked up your first rifle or have had one in the safe for years, this course gives you the foundation to run it safely and confidently. You'll cover everything from safe handling and fundamentals to zeroing, reloading, and live fire drills — all with hands-on coaching from NTAC instructors. No prior experience required.

What You'll Master

Skills You'll Take With You

✓
Safe handling, loading, and unloading your rifle or carbine in any situation
✓
Proper stance, grip, and trigger control for accurate and consistent shooting
✓
Zeroing your sights or optic and understanding how your rifle shoots at distance
✓
Magazine changes and clearing common malfunctions under range conditions
✓
Foundational shooting positions and live fire drills with real instructor feedback
Who This Is For

Starting From Zero? You're In the Right Place.

This course is designed for complete beginners — no prior shooting experience required. If you own a rifle and don't know where to start, or you've shot a few times but never had formal instruction, this is your entry point.

NTAC instructors meet you where you are and build you up from the ground floor, safely and without judgment.

What to Expect

Fundamentals First. Range Time Second. Leave Confident.

  • Classroom session covering safety rules, parts, and how your rifle works
  • One-on-one feedback on your stance, grip, and trigger press
  • Live fire practice with real coaching — not just watching
  • Zeroing your optic or iron sights during the course
  • Plan on approximately 100 rounds of live fire
