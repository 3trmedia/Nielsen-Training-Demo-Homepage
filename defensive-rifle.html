Defensive Rifle Class

Can You Run Your Rifle When It Matters Most?

Reserve My Spot

This is NTAC's most advanced rifle course — designed for experienced shooters who are ready to train for real-world defensive application. Defensive Rifle puts your skills in motion: scenario-based live fire, cover and concealment, team movement, and the legal framework for using deadly force. Not quite there yet? Complete Rifle Proficiency first.

What You'll Master

Skills You'll Take With You

✓
Running your rifle effectively in real-world defensive scenarios under pressure
✓
Moving and shooting accurately from cover and concealment in any direction
✓
Magazine changes and malfunction clearances without breaking situational awareness
✓
Home defense and vehicle scenarios practiced in live fire environments
✓
The legal framework for use of deadly force — when justified and what follows
Who This Is For

NTAC's Most Advanced Rifle Course.

Experienced shooters who have completed Rifle Proficiency. You should be able to confidently run your rifle on the move and execute rapid magazine changes before attending. This is not a beginner or intermediate course.

If you've been training seriously and want to take your skills into real-world defensive application, this is where that happens.

What to Expect

Live Fire. Real Scenarios. No Shortcuts.

  • Scenario-based live fire drills from the very first session
  • Home defense and vehicle engagement situations on the range
  • Legal framework for use of force covered as a practical tool, not a lecture
  • Approximately 400 rounds — come ready to work hard
  • You will not be standing still at any point in this course

What to Bring

  • Rifle or carbine with a sling
  • At least 3 magazines for your rifle
  • 400 rounds — no armor-piercing or steel core ammo
  • Eye protection
  • Hearing protection
  • Closed-toe shoes, long pants, high neckline shirt
